About me

I am a very passionate and caring person. I am driven by success and the idea of a great and promising future for not only myself but for my family as well. I grew up in Willis, Texas and later moved to Houston where I met my partner. We moved to San Francisco and I became an EMT.

I started learning American Sign Language in early 2018 and currently still. I have found that language to be beautiful as well as useful in situations.

I am currently in this bootcamp to learn about web development. Working as an EMT I realized that I enjoy solving puzzels and fixing things, and it's also very clear that the future is tech and I need to hop on board before its too late. A week in and i'm a little lost but catching up everyday and loving every bit of it.

Usually my hobbies include spending time with my family, video games, skateboarding, and relaxing. My new hobbies for the past couple of months have been this bootcamp. So coding, coding, and more coding have been my new enjoyable hobbies! I work hard so my dog can live a happy life.
